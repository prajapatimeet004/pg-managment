## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific** - More details = better responses
   - ❌ "Show complaints" 
   - ✅ "Show urgent complaints in Sunshine PG from last week"

2. **Use Natural Language** - AI understands multiple formats
   - "Who has overdue rent?" 
   - "List delinquent tenants"
   - "Show me payment defaults"

3. **Ask for Actions** - AI can execute tasks
   - "Send reminders"
   - "Register a tenant"
   - "Mark complaint as resolved"

4. **Request Analysis** - AI provides insights
   - "What's our biggest issue?"
   - "Which property underperforms?"
   - "Show trends"

5. **Ask for Summaries** - Get quick overviews
   - "Brief update on business"
   - "Key metrics"
   - "What needs attention?"
